Our Values

We create flavorsome, traditional dishes using fresh, local ingredients.

The Local is the best place in Miami to enjoy great locally sourced food,
18 unique and rotating craft beers on tap, and a carefully curated selection of small-batch
craft wine and spirits in a warm and welcoming pub environment.

Our chalkboard is updated daily with seasonal food and
cocktail specials as we take pride in offering quality,
responsibly-sourced options to our customers.
Truly great food is a combination of quality ingredients, creativity, and attention to detail.
